Welcome
We are a multi-ethnic and intergenerational church located in Bells Corners that loves God, teaches the Bible, serves people and cares for the world.

We meet on Zoom/Youtube for worship on Sundays @ 11:15 AM.
You can find us here.

We Believe

There is one Triune god.
that Jesus is Lord and We frame our lives around Him.
The Bible is God's revelation for us and is authoritative.

We affirm the faith laid out in the Nicene Creed and the statement of faith of our denomination, the Christian and Missionary Alliance in Canada.

Learn more about our story.

Sundays Together


We meet together 11:15 AM for Sunday Service.

Our Sunday service centres around worship music, prayer, collection of offering, and hearing from God's Word. Everyone is welcome!
